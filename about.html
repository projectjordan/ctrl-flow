About The Blog

Ctrl+Flow serves as a dedication to my overwhelming love for all things that excite me;
Whether that be the newest video games and the trends that follow it, the cutting-edge ideas in tech, the thrill-seeking stunts I wish I had the guts to pull off, or the music that I love to listen to.
I started my journey in web design and programming in late 2023,
and while I have much to learn, I want for this project to be something I can always look back on and cherish, as well as something that I can continuously improve upon, much like I plan on doing with myself.
Thank you for joining me here and reading this - I hope you're around to see some of the cool things I want to build in the future!
